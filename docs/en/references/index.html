References & data sources

DIAAS values on this site come from the peer-reviewed literature below; the amino-acid values shown are from USDA FoodData Central. The national and regional food-composition databases listed under data sources are additional authoritative references for the protein and amino-acid composition of foods. Existing whole-food DIAAS values are representative figures compiled from Mathai 2017, Bailey 2020, and FAO 2013.
