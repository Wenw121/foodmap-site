Protein-quality guides

Curated lists of foods by protein quality (DIAAS), food group, and methionine content — a quick way to find complete proteins, the best plant proteins, and more.

  • Complete proteins: foods with DIAAS 100 or higher 34

    Foods whose protein scores DIAAS 100 or above — the FAO's “excellent quality” tier, meaning they meet adult essential amino-acid needs on their own.

  • High-protein plant foods 20

    Plant foods ranked by protein per 100 g, with DIAAS and methionine for comparison.

  • DIAAS of legumes and beans 16

    Protein quality (DIAAS) and limiting amino acids of beans, lentils, peas, and soy foods — most are limited by the sulfur amino acids.

  • DIAAS of grains and cereals 11

    Protein quality of rice, wheat, oats, and other cereals — usually limited by lysine.

  • Lower-methionine foods (per gram of protein) 36

    Foods in the lower third for methionine per gram of protein. A neutral description of composition, not a health rating.
