Potato

马铃薯（土豆）

Plant · vegetables/algae Lower methionine

Potato provides 2.05 g of protein per 100 g, making it a lower-protein food by weight — about 2.0 g in a typical 100 g serving. Its protein-quality score (DIAAS) is 100, which the FAO classes as an excellent-quality protein. It supplies all essential amino acids in adequate amounts, so it counts as a complete protein on its own. Measured per gram of protein, it is low in methionine (15.6 mg/g) and provides 52.2 mg/g of lysine.
