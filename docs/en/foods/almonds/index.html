Almonds

杏仁（巴旦木）

Plant · nuts Lower methionine

Almonds provides 21.15 g of protein per 100 g, making it a high-protein food — about 6.3 g in a typical 30 g serving. Its protein-quality score (DIAAS) is 40, which is below the FAO threshold for a quality claim, and its first limiting amino acid is lysine. Measured per gram of protein, it is low in methionine (7.4 mg/g) and provides 26.9 mg/g of lysine.
