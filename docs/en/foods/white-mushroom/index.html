White mushroom

白蘑菇

Plant · vegetables/algae Lower methionine

White mushroom provides 3.09 g of protein per 100 g, making it a lower-protein food by weight — about 3.1 g in a typical 100 g serving. No DIAAS has been published for White mushroom, so its protein quality is not scored here; the full amino-acid profile is shown below. Measured per gram of protein, it is low in methionine (10.0 mg/g) and provides 34.6 mg/g of lysine.
