Soy protein isolate

大豆分离蛋白

Plant · legumes Lower methionine

Soy protein isolate provides 88.32 g of protein per 100 g, making it a high-protein food — about 26.5 g in a typical 30 g serving. Its protein-quality score (DIAAS) is 90, which the FAO classes as a high-quality protein, and its first limiting amino acid is the sulfur amino acids (methionine and cysteine). Measured per gram of protein, it is low in methionine (12.8 mg/g) and provides 60.3 mg/g of lysine.
