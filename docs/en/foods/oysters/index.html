Oysters

牡蛎

Animal · fish/seafood Intermediate methionine

Oysters provides 5.22 g of protein per 100 g, making it a lower-protein food by weight — about 7.8 g in a typical 150 g serving. Its protein-quality score (DIAAS) is 95, which the FAO classes as a high-quality protein. Measured per gram of protein, it is moderate in methionine (22.6 mg/g) and provides 74.7 mg/g of lysine.
