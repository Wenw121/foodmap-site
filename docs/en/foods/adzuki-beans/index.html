Adzuki beans

红豆

Plant · legumes Lower methionine

Adzuki beans provides 7.52 g of protein per 100 g, making it a lower-protein food by weight — about 7.5 g in a typical 100 g serving. No DIAAS has been published for Adzuki beans, so its protein quality is not scored here; the full amino-acid profile is shown below. Measured per gram of protein, it is low in methionine (10.5 mg/g) and provides 75.4 mg/g of lysine.
