Mackerel

Animal · fish/seafood Higher methionine

Mackerel provides 18.6 g of protein per 100 g, making it a moderate-protein food — about 27.9 g in a typical 150 g serving. Its protein-quality score (DIAAS) is 104, which the FAO classes as an excellent-quality protein. It supplies all essential amino acids in adequate amounts, so it counts as a complete protein on its own. Measured per gram of protein, it is high in methionine (29.6 mg/g) and provides 91.8 mg/g of lysine.
