Natto

纳豆

Plant · legumes Lower methionine

Natto provides 19.4 g of protein per 100 g, making it a moderate-protein food — about 19.4 g in a typical 100 g serving. Its protein-quality score (DIAAS) is 91, which the FAO classes as a high-quality protein, and its first limiting amino acid is the sulfur amino acids (methionine and cysteine). Measured per gram of protein, it is low in methionine (10.7 mg/g) and provides 59.0 mg/g of lysine.
