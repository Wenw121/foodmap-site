Sweet potato

红薯

Plant · vegetables/algae Intermediate methionine

Sweet potato provides 1.57 g of protein per 100 g, making it a lower-protein food by weight — about 1.6 g in a typical 100 g serving. No DIAAS has been published for Sweet potato, so its protein quality is not scored here; the full amino-acid profile is shown below. Measured per gram of protein, it is moderate in methionine (18.5 mg/g) and provides 42.0 mg/g of lysine.
