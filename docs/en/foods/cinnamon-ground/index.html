Cinnamon (ground)

Spice · seasoning Intermediate methionine

Cinnamon (ground) provides 3.99 g of protein per 100 g, making it a lower-protein food by weight — about 0.1 g in a typical 2 g serving. No DIAAS has been published for Cinnamon (ground), so its protein quality is not scored here; the full amino-acid profile is shown below. Measured per gram of protein, it is moderate in methionine (19.5 mg/g) and provides 60.9 mg/g of lysine.
