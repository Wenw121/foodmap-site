Gelatin

明胶

Special · collagen Lower methionine

Gelatin provides 85.6 g of protein per 100 g, making it a high-protein food — about 8.6 g in a typical 10 g serving. Its protein-quality score (DIAAS) is 2, which is below the FAO threshold for a quality claim, and its first limiting amino acid is tryptophan. Measured per gram of protein, it is low in methionine (7.1 mg/g) and provides 40.4 mg/g of lysine.
