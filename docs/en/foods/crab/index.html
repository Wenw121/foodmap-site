Crab

蟹

Animal · fish/seafood Higher methionine

Crab provides 18.06 g of protein per 100 g, making it a moderate-protein food — about 27.1 g in a typical 150 g serving. Its protein-quality score (DIAAS) is 100, which the FAO classes as an excellent-quality protein. It supplies all essential amino acids in adequate amounts, so it counts as a complete protein on its own. Measured per gram of protein, it is high in methionine (28.1 mg/g) and provides 87.0 mg/g of lysine.
