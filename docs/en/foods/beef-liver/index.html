Beef liver

牛肝

Animal · red meat Higher methionine

Beef liver provides 20.36 g of protein per 100 g, making it a high-protein food — about 30.5 g in a typical 150 g serving. Its protein-quality score (DIAAS) is 112, which the FAO classes as an excellent-quality protein. It supplies all essential amino acids in adequate amounts, so it counts as a complete protein on its own. Measured per gram of protein, it is high in methionine (26.7 mg/g) and provides 78.9 mg/g of lysine.
