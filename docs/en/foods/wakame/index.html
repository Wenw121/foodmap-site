Wakame seaweed

裙带菜

Plant · vegetables/algae Intermediate methionine

Wakame seaweed provides 3.03 g of protein per 100 g, making it a lower-protein food by weight — about 0.3 g in a typical 10 g serving. No DIAAS has been published for Wakame seaweed, so its protein quality is not scored here; the full amino-acid profile is shown below. Measured per gram of protein, it is moderate in methionine (20.8 mg/g) and provides 37.0 mg/g of lysine.
