Flathead

鲬鱼

Animal · fish/seafood Higher methionine

Flathead provides 21.1 g of protein per 100 g, making it a high-protein food — about 31.6 g in a typical 150 g serving. No DIAAS has been published for Flathead, so its protein quality is not scored here; the full amino-acid profile is shown below. Measured per gram of protein, it is high in methionine (30.3 mg/g) and provides 76.2 mg/g of lysine.
