Tempeh

天贝

Plant · legumes Lower methionine

Tempeh provides 20.29 g of protein per 100 g, making it a high-protein food — about 20.3 g in a typical 100 g serving. Its protein-quality score (DIAAS) is 91, which the FAO classes as a high-quality protein, and its first limiting amino acid is the sulfur amino acids (methionine and cysteine). Measured per gram of protein, it is low in methionine (8.6 mg/g) and provides 44.8 mg/g of lysine.
