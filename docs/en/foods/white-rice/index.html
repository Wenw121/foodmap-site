White rice

白米

Plant · grains Intermediate methionine

White rice provides 7.13 g of protein per 100 g, making it a lower-protein food by weight — about 3.6 g in a typical 50 g serving. Its protein-quality score (DIAAS) is 60, which is below the FAO threshold for a quality claim, and its first limiting amino acid is lysine. Measured per gram of protein, it is moderate in methionine (23.6 mg/g) and provides 36.2 mg/g of lysine.
