Green peas

Plant · legumes Lower methionine

Green peas provides 5.42 g of protein per 100 g, making it a lower-protein food by weight — about 5.4 g in a typical 100 g serving. Its protein-quality score (DIAAS) is 70, which is below the FAO threshold for a quality claim, and its first limiting amino acid is the sulfur amino acids (methionine and cysteine). Measured per gram of protein, it is low in methionine (15.1 mg/g) and provides 58.5 mg/g of lysine.
