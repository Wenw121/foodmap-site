Chickpeas

Plant · legumes Lower methionine

Chickpeas provides 20.47 g of protein per 100 g, making it a high-protein food — about 20.5 g in a typical 100 g serving. Its protein-quality score (DIAAS) is 83, which the FAO classes as a high-quality protein, and its first limiting amino acid is the sulfur amino acids (methionine and cysteine). Measured per gram of protein, it is low in methionine (13.2 mg/g) and provides 67.3 mg/g of lysine.
