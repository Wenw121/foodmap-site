Soy milk

豆浆

Plant · legumes Lower methionine

Soy milk provides 3.27 g of protein per 100 g, making it a lower-protein food by weight — about 7.8 g in a typical 240 g serving. No DIAAS has been published for Soy milk, so its protein quality is not scored here; the full amino-acid profile is shown below. Measured per gram of protein, it is low in methionine (8.3 mg/g) and provides 40.1 mg/g of lysine.
