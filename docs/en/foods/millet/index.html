Millet

小米

Plant · grains Intermediate methionine

Millet provides 11.02 g of protein per 100 g, making it a moderate-protein food — about 5.5 g in a typical 50 g serving. Its protein-quality score (DIAAS) is 50, which is below the FAO threshold for a quality claim, and its first limiting amino acid is lysine. Measured per gram of protein, it is moderate in methionine (20.1 mg/g) and provides 19.2 mg/g of lysine.
