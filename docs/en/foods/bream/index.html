Bream

黑鲷

Animal · fish/seafood Higher methionine

Bream provides 19.6 g of protein per 100 g, making it a moderate-protein food — about 29.4 g in a typical 150 g serving. No DIAAS has been published for Bream, so its protein quality is not scored here; the full amino-acid profile is shown below. Measured per gram of protein, it is high in methionine (29.5 mg/g) and provides 75.7 mg/g of lysine.
