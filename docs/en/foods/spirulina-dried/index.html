Spirulina (dried)

螺旋藻（干）

Plant · vegetables/algae Intermediate methionine

Spirulina (dried) provides 57.47 g of protein per 100 g, making it a high-protein food — about 4.0 g in a typical 7 g serving. Its protein-quality score (DIAAS) is 60, which is below the FAO threshold for a quality claim, and its first limiting amino acid is the sulfur amino acids (methionine and cysteine). Measured per gram of protein, it is moderate in methionine (20.0 mg/g) and provides 52.6 mg/g of lysine.
