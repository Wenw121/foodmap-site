Wheat (hard)

Plant · grains Intermediate methionine

Wheat (hard) provides 12.61 g of protein per 100 g, making it a moderate-protein food — about 6.3 g in a typical 50 g serving. Its protein-quality score (DIAAS) is 45, which is below the FAO threshold for a quality claim, and its first limiting amino acid is lysine. Measured per gram of protein, it is moderate in methionine (15.9 mg/g) and provides 26.6 mg/g of lysine.
