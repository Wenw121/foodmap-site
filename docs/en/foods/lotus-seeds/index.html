Lotus seeds

莲子

Plant · seeds Intermediate methionine

Lotus seeds provides 15.41 g of protein per 100 g, making it a moderate-protein food — about 3.1 g in a typical 20 g serving. No DIAAS has been published for Lotus seeds, so its protein quality is not scored here; the full amino-acid profile is shown below. Measured per gram of protein, it is moderate in methionine (17.3 mg/g) and provides 63.9 mg/g of lysine.
