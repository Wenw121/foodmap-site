Scallops

扇贝

Animal · fish/seafood Intermediate methionine

Scallops provides 12.06 g of protein per 100 g, making it a moderate-protein food — about 18.1 g in a typical 150 g serving. Its protein-quality score (DIAAS) is 100, which the FAO classes as an excellent-quality protein. It supplies all essential amino acids in adequate amounts, so it counts as a complete protein on its own. Measured per gram of protein, it is moderate in methionine (23.7 mg/g) and provides 61.3 mg/g of lysine.
