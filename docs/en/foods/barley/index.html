Barley

Plant · grains Intermediate methionine

Barley provides 9.91 g of protein per 100 g, making it a lower-protein food by weight — about 5.0 g in a typical 50 g serving. Its protein-quality score (DIAAS) is 55, which is below the FAO threshold for a quality claim, and its first limiting amino acid is lysine. Measured per gram of protein, it is moderate in methionine (19.2 mg/g) and provides 37.2 mg/g of lysine.
