Brazil nuts

巴西坚果

Plant · nuts Higher methionine

Brazil nuts provides 14.32 g of protein per 100 g, making it a moderate-protein food — about 4.3 g in a typical 30 g serving. Its protein-quality score (DIAAS) is 41, which is below the FAO threshold for a quality claim, and its first limiting amino acid is lysine. Measured per gram of protein, it is high in methionine (78.5 mg/g) and provides 34.2 mg/g of lysine.
