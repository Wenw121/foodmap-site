Soba noodles

荞麦面

Plant · grains Lower methionine

Soba noodles provides 14.38 g of protein per 100 g, making it a moderate-protein food — about 7.2 g in a typical 50 g serving. Its protein-quality score (DIAAS) is 68, which is below the FAO threshold for a quality claim, and its first limiting amino acid is lysine. Measured per gram of protein, it is low in methionine (14.2 mg/g) and provides 42.2 mg/g of lysine.
