Tofu

豆腐

Plant · legumes Lower methionine

Tofu provides 17.27 g of protein per 100 g, making it a moderate-protein food — about 17.3 g in a typical 100 g serving. Its protein-quality score (DIAAS) is 56, which is below the FAO threshold for a quality claim, and its first limiting amino acid is the sulfur amino acids (methionine and cysteine). Measured per gram of protein, it is low in methionine (12.2 mg/g) and provides 51.1 mg/g of lysine.
