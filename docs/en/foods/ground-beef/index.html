Ground beef

牛肉糜

Animal · red meat Higher methionine

Ground beef provides 14.35 g of protein per 100 g, making it a moderate-protein food — about 21.5 g in a typical 150 g serving. Its protein-quality score (DIAAS) is 111, which the FAO classes as an excellent-quality protein. It supplies all essential amino acids in adequate amounts, so it counts as a complete protein on its own. Measured per gram of protein, it is high in methionine (25.7 mg/g) and provides 82.8 mg/g of lysine.
