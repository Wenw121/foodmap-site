Squid (calamari)

Animal · fish/seafood Higher methionine

Squid (calamari) provides 16.7 g of protein per 100 g, making it a moderate-protein food — about 25.1 g in a typical 150 g serving. No DIAAS has been published for Squid (calamari), so its protein quality is not scored here; the full amino-acid profile is shown below. Measured per gram of protein, it is high in methionine (25.7 mg/g) and provides 63.5 mg/g of lysine.
