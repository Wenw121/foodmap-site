Abalone

鲍鱼

Animal · fish/seafood Intermediate methionine

Abalone provides 17.1 g of protein per 100 g, making it a moderate-protein food — about 25.6 g in a typical 150 g serving. No DIAAS has been published for Abalone, so its protein quality is not scored here; the full amino-acid profile is shown below. Measured per gram of protein, it is moderate in methionine (22.6 mg/g) and provides 74.7 mg/g of lysine.
