Yam

Plant · vegetables/algae Lower methionine

Yam provides 1.53 g of protein per 100 g, making it a lower-protein food by weight — about 1.5 g in a typical 100 g serving. No DIAAS has been published for Yam, so its protein quality is not scored here; the full amino-acid profile is shown below. Measured per gram of protein, it is low in methionine (13.7 mg/g) and provides 38.6 mg/g of lysine.
