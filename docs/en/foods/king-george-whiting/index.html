King George whiting

乔治王鱚

Animal · fish/seafood Higher methionine

King George whiting provides 19.8 g of protein per 100 g, making it a moderate-protein food — about 29.7 g in a typical 150 g serving. No DIAAS has been published for King George whiting, so its protein quality is not scored here; the full amino-acid profile is shown below. Measured per gram of protein, it is high in methionine (29.7 mg/g) and provides 73.9 mg/g of lysine.
