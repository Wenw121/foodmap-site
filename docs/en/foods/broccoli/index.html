Broccoli

西兰花

Plant · vegetables/algae Lower methionine

Broccoli provides 2.82 g of protein per 100 g, making it a lower-protein food by weight — about 2.8 g in a typical 100 g serving. No DIAAS has been published for Broccoli, so its protein quality is not scored here; the full amino-acid profile is shown below. Measured per gram of protein, it is low in methionine (13.5 mg/g) and provides 47.9 mg/g of lysine.
