Chicken breast

Animal · poultry Higher methionine

Chicken breast provides 20.85 g of protein per 100 g, making it a high-protein food — about 31.3 g in a typical 150 g serving. Its protein-quality score (DIAAS) is 108, which the FAO classes as an excellent-quality protein. It supplies all essential amino acids in adequate amounts, so it counts as a complete protein on its own. Measured per gram of protein, it is high in methionine (27.0 mg/g) and provides 82.7 mg/g of lysine.
