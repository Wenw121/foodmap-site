Corn

Plant · grains Intermediate methionine

Corn provides 9.42 g of protein per 100 g, making it a lower-protein food by weight — about 4.7 g in a typical 50 g serving. Its protein-quality score (DIAAS) is 42, which is below the FAO threshold for a quality claim, and its first limiting amino acid is lysine. Measured per gram of protein, it is moderate in methionine (20.9 mg/g) and provides 28.1 mg/g of lysine.
