Enoki mushroom

金针菇

Plant · vegetables/algae Lower methionine

Enoki mushroom provides 2.66 g of protein per 100 g, making it a lower-protein food by weight — about 2.7 g in a typical 100 g serving. No DIAAS has been published for Enoki mushroom, so its protein quality is not scored here; the full amino-acid profile is shown below. Measured per gram of protein, it is low in methionine (11.3 mg/g) and provides 48.9 mg/g of lysine.
