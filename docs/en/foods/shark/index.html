Shark

Animal · fish/seafood Intermediate methionine

Shark provides 24.6 g of protein per 100 g, making it a high-protein food — about 36.9 g in a typical 150 g serving. No DIAAS has been published for Shark, so its protein quality is not scored here; the full amino-acid profile is shown below. Measured per gram of protein, it is moderate in methionine (21.8 mg/g) and provides 68.9 mg/g of lysine.
