Mung bean sprouts

绿豆芽

Plant · vegetables/algae Lower methionine

Mung bean sprouts provides 3.04 g of protein per 100 g, making it a lower-protein food by weight — about 3.0 g in a typical 100 g serving. No DIAAS has been published for Mung bean sprouts, so its protein quality is not scored here; the full amino-acid profile is shown below. Measured per gram of protein, it is low in methionine (11.2 mg/g) and provides 54.6 mg/g of lysine.
