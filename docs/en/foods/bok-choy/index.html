Bok choy (pak choi)

青江菜

Plant · vegetables/algae Lower methionine

Bok choy (pak choi) provides 1.5 g of protein per 100 g, making it a lower-protein food by weight — about 1.5 g in a typical 100 g serving. No DIAAS has been published for Bok choy (pak choi), so its protein quality is not scored here; the full amino-acid profile is shown below. Measured per gram of protein, it is low in methionine (6.0 mg/g) and provides 59.3 mg/g of lysine.
