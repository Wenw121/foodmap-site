Pumpkin seeds

Plant · seeds Intermediate methionine

Pumpkin seeds provides 30.23 g of protein per 100 g, making it a high-protein food — about 6.0 g in a typical 20 g serving. Its protein-quality score (DIAAS) is 50, which is below the FAO threshold for a quality claim, and its first limiting amino acid is lysine. Measured per gram of protein, it is moderate in methionine (19.9 mg/g) and provides 40.9 mg/g of lysine.
