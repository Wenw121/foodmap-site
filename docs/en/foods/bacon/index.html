Bacon

培根

Animal · processed meat Intermediate methionine

Bacon provides 35.73 g of protein per 100 g, making it a high-protein food — about 17.9 g in a typical 50 g serving. Its protein-quality score (DIAAS) is 100, which the FAO classes as an excellent-quality protein. It supplies all essential amino acids in adequate amounts, so it counts as a complete protein on its own. Measured per gram of protein, it is moderate in methionine (22.3 mg/g) and provides 83.0 mg/g of lysine.
