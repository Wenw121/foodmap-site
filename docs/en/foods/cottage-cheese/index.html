Cottage cheese

茅屋芝士

Animal · whey/dairy Higher methionine

Cottage cheese provides 12.39 g of protein per 100 g, making it a moderate-protein food — about 3.7 g in a typical 30 g serving. Its protein-quality score (DIAAS) is 118, which the FAO classes as an excellent-quality protein. It supplies all essential amino acids in adequate amounts, so it counts as a complete protein on its own. Measured per gram of protein, it is high in methionine (30.1 mg/g) and provides 80.9 mg/g of lysine.
