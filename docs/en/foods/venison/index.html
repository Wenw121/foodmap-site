Venison (lean)

Animal · red meat Higher methionine

Venison (lean) provides 23.5 g of protein per 100 g, making it a high-protein food — about 35.2 g in a typical 150 g serving. No DIAAS has been published for Venison (lean), so its protein quality is not scored here; the full amino-acid profile is shown below. Measured per gram of protein, it is high in methionine (27.2 mg/g) and provides 91.3 mg/g of lysine.
