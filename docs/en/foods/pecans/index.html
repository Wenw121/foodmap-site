Pecans

碧根果（美国山核桃）

Plant · nuts Intermediate methionine

Pecans provides 9.17 g of protein per 100 g, making it a lower-protein food by weight — about 2.8 g in a typical 30 g serving. Its protein-quality score (DIAAS) is 40, which is below the FAO threshold for a quality claim, and its first limiting amino acid is lysine. Measured per gram of protein, it is moderate in methionine (20.0 mg/g) and provides 31.3 mg/g of lysine.
