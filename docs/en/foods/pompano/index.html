Pompano

鲳鱼

Animal · fish/seafood Higher methionine

Pompano provides 18.48 g of protein per 100 g, making it a moderate-protein food — about 27.7 g in a typical 150 g serving. No DIAAS has been published for Pompano, so its protein quality is not scored here; the full amino-acid profile is shown below. Measured per gram of protein, it is high in methionine (29.6 mg/g) and provides 91.8 mg/g of lysine.
